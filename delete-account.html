Data Deletion

Delete Your Account

To permanently delete your **Oppad** account and all associated transit data, please follow these steps within the app:

1

Go to your Profile page.

2

Select Delete Account at the bottom.

3

Type DELETE to confirm.

Note:

This action is irreversible. All sightings, badges, and favorite routes will be permanently removed from our database.

Alternative Deletion Method

If you no longer have the app installed and wish to request data deletion, please email us at ozunuoden@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.
