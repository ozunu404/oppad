Oppad Account Deletion

In accordance with Google Play policies, users of Oppad have the right to request the deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request deletion:

  • Click the button below to draft a request from your registered email.

  • Include your username/email used for Oppad.

  • Our support team will verify and process your request within 48-72 hours.

Email Support Team

Data Retention & Deletion Policy:

Deleted Data:

Your account profile (email, password hash), user ID, and private message history will be permanently erased from our Supabase database.

Kept Data:

Public bus sightings or reports may be de-identified and retained for community historical accuracy but will no longer be linked to your identity.

Retention:

Once processed, data is immediately removed from active servers. Residual data in encrypted backups may persist for up to 30 days before being overwritten.
